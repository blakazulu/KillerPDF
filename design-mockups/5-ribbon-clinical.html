Asset Acquisition Agreement

DOC-2026-0418 · EXECUTION COPY · 12 PP

This Agreement is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between the parties identified below, who agree to the terms set forth herein with respect to the acquisition of the assets described in Schedule A.

Definitions

For purposes of this Agreement, capitalized terms have the meanings given to them in this Section. "Closing" means the consummation of the transactions contemplated by this Agreement.

"Purchased Assets" means all right, title, and interest in the assets, properties, and rights set forth on the attached schedules, free and clear of all encumbrances.

Purchase & Sale

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, at the Closing the Seller shall sell and the Buyer shall purchase the Purchased Assets for the consideration described herein.
